Interactive Comic

Description:

For this assignment, our group of three was tasked with creating an Interactive comic in the Unity engine. Our theme was randomness in the universe so we decided to create a branching story that had eight different endings. My contributions to this comic included creating the intro cinematic animation, coding a minigame, and art for over 24 panels. The animation and comic panels were all created in Blender.
